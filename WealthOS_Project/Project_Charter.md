## EXECUTIVE SUMMMARY
* WealthOS is a forward-looking financial decision intelligence platform designed to help individuals make confident, well-informed financial choices before they commit to them.
* Most personal finance tools focus on the past tracking what you've already spent, earned or saved. WealthOS focuses on the future with data-driven simulations, forecasts, and personalized recommendations.
* This document formally authorizes the WealthOS project, defines its scope, objectives and strategic retionale, and establishes the foundation for all subsequent analysis and delivery phases.
  
## PROBLEM STATEMENT
* Most personal finance applications help users track spending, savings, investments, and account balances, providing visibility into their current financial situation. While users can see their financial data, they often lack the tools to evaluate how major life decisions—such as buying a home, changing careers, or planning for retirement which will affect their long-term financial goals.  As a result, financial decisions are frequently made based on intuition, fragmented information, or manual calculations, increasing the risk of poor financial outcomes, missed goals, and financial uncertainty.
* WealthOS aims to address this gap by providing a Financial Decision Intelligence Platform that combines financial data, forecasting, scenario simulation, and personalized recommendations to help users make informed financial decisions with greater confidence.
